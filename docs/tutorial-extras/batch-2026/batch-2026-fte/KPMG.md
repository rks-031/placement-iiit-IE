# KPMG Interview Experience  

**Article by:** Sambit Mohapatra  
**Company Name:** KPMG    
**Job Role:** GenAI Engineer    
  

---

## Online Assessment Round  

**Duration:** 1 hour 30 minutes  
**Mode:** Online  

### Sections  
- **Spoken English:**  
  4 questions, each with a 1-minute timer to evaluate speaking skills.  

- **MCQ Section:**  
  Around 30 questions (Aptitude + English grammar & vocabulary).  

- **DSA Section:**  
  2 medium-level coding problems based on:  
  - Stack  
  - Greedy approach  

---

## Technical Interview  

**Mode:** Virtual/Online  
**Candidates Shortlisted:** 11  

### Discussion Flow  

- **Introduction**  
  Introduced myself with background, projects, and achievements.  
  Interviewer asked my preferred programming language; I mentioned **C++**.  

- **DSA Questions**  
  1. **Valid Parenthesis Problem**  
     - Solved using **Stack**.  
  2. **Find all substrings of a given length**  
     - Solved using **Backtracking**.  

- **Project Discussion**  
  Questions on:  
  - Technologies and frameworks used  
  - Implementation details of **ReactJS**  
  - Project workflows and contributions  

- **Python & GenAI**  
  Asked basics of:  
  - Python  
  - Large Language Models (LLMs)  
  - GenAI fundamentals  

### Conclusion  
Interview ended with an overview of:  
- The job role  
- Work culture at KPMG  

---

## HR Interview  

There was **no separate HR round**.  
Final selection was based solely on performance in the technical interview.  
**5 students were selected.**

---

## Key Takeaways  

- Be confident with your fundamentals  
- Strong command over basic to medium-level DSA  
- Deep understanding of your projects  
- Basic awareness and willingness to work with emerging technologies (GenAI, Agentic AI, etc.)
