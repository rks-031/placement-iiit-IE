#### Question 2
You are given:
- A dictionary of valid words  
- A message string  

For each word in the message:

**Input:**

## Technical Interview Round

**Mode:** Virtual (Proctored by Placement Cell Team)

* The interview started with a **self-introduction**.
* I was asked about my **preferred programming language** (I answered C++).

### OOPS Questions

* What is encapsulation?
* What is abstraction?
* What are abstract classes and interfaces?
* What are virtual functions?

### Logical & Coding Questions

1. **Logic-Based Problem (Gym Scenario):**

   * If a person visits the gym on **any one day of a week**, they are charged for the **entire week**.
   * Input: An array of swipe days (days a person visits the gym)
   * Output: Number of weeks the person will be charged for

2. **Coding Question:**

   * Write a program to **reverse a string**.

## HR Round

**Mode:** Virtual (Proctored by Placement Cell Team)

### Questions Asked

* Why do you want **HSBC** as your employer?
* Explain a project you have worked on.
* Describe a time when you took a **leadership responsibility** and the challenges you faced.

* Are you comfortable with a **hybrid work mode**?

## Final Note

* Make sure you have **strong fundamentals**.
* Do not give up on **logic-building questions** during the interview.
* Even if you cannot complete the full code, **clearly explain your approach**.
* Try to **interact with the interviewer** as much as possible, even while coding.
