## Technical Round 1

The interview began with my introduction, followed by a detailed discussion of my projects. I was asked to explain my project thoroughly, and the interviewer asked several follow-up questions based on it.

### Project & Technical Discussion
- Explanation of project workflow  
- Questions related to project design and implementation  
- Real-world applicability of the project  
- My individual contribution  

### Core Concepts & Questions
- Structured, semi-structured, and unstructured data  
- Python basics  
- One **moderate-level SQL query**  
- Mathematical sequence questions (predicting the next numbers)  

### Data Science & ML Focus
- Data preprocessing techniques  
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)  
- Real-life industrial problem-solving approach  
- Choosing between classification vs regression models  
- Practical applicability of ML models in production scenarios  

This round was more **industry-oriented** rather than purely DSA or theory-based.

---

## HR Round

The HR round was mostly conversational and focused on understanding my background and personality.

### Topics Discussed
- Personal and family background  
- Academic journey and JEE experience  
- Projects and technical skills  
- Relocation preferences  
- General career-related discussion
