

# Mindfire Interview Experience  
**Article by**: Sashikanta Pattanaik  
**Company Name**: Mindfire  
**Job Description**: Associate Software Engineer

---

## Online Assessment Round

**Mode:** Online (Conducted at Mindfire Office on their PC)

### Round Details

* **No. of MCQ Questions:** No MCQs
* **No. of DSA Questions:** 5 (Easy to Medium level)
* **No. of Aptitude Questions:** None

### Selection Criteria

* Total score was calculated based on all questions.
* Final selection was done purely on the **overall score**.

---

## Technical Round 1

**Mode:** Offline
**Duration:** 1 hour

The interview started with a **self-introduction**, followed by:

* One **DSA question**
* Then **resume-based questions** (mainly projects)

### DSA Question

* Find **Nth factorial using recursion**
* Explain the **time and space complexity**

### Project-Based Questions

* Write the code and explain the working of modules used in your database.
* Difference between **Authentication and Authorization** and how it is implemented in your project.
* Explain the **Razorpay payment integration flow**.
* What is **indexing in databases**?
* What is **GridFS** and why is it used? Compare it with **Cloudinary**.
* How to **optimize backend performance** using modular APIs and proper DB schema.

## HR Round

### Questions Asked

* Family background and preferred job locations.
* Why do you want to join **Mindfire**?
* Tell about a **real-world problem-solving experience** (not necessarily technical).
* Which **domains** are you interested in and why?

### Questions I Asked Them

* What will be my **roles and responsibilities**?
* If I want to shift to another **domain/tech stack/project**, what is the procedure and exposure?
* What is the **internship duration** and **PPO conversion process**?
