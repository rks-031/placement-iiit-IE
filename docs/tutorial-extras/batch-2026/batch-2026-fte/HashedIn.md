# HashedIn Technologies Interview Experience  

**Company Name**: HashedIn Technologies  
**Article by**: Ranjan Kumar (B522048)  
**Role**: Trainee Engineer  

---

## Online Assessment Round  

**Duration:** 1 hour 30 minutes  
**Content:**  
- **No. of DSA questions:** 3  

### Questions Asked:  
1. You are given an array of strings `arr[]` containing `N` words.  
   Your task is to sort the characters of each word in alphabetical order and return the updated array.  

2. A problem similar to **LeetCode 974: Subarray Sums Divisible by K**,  
   where you are given an integer array `nums` and an integer `k`.  
   The task was to find the total number of subarrays whose sum is divisible by `k`.  

3. A problem similar to:  
   You are given a string `s` and an integer `k`.  
   You can split the string into substrings and change at most `k` characters in each substring.  
   Count the number of substrings that can be transformed into a palindrome with these changes.  

---

## Technical Round 1 (1 Hour)

### Questions & Discussion  
- **Introduction**  
- **Reverse Linked List (LeetCode problem)**  
- **Best Time to Buy and Sell Stock IV (LeetCode problem)**  

### System Design Discussion  
I was asked to design a system that:  
- Generates pairs from two arrays efficiently.  
- Fetches additional data for each pair from a database using batch or asynchronous queries.  
- Handles large-scale data with caching, load balancing, and distributed databases.  
- Ensures data integrity, consistency, and proper error handling.  

---

## Technical Round 2 (1 Hour)

### Questions & Discussion  
- **Introduction**  
- Family background related questions.  

I was then asked to **design an ER Diagram for Zomato** with at least **8 entities**, including:  
`Users`, `Restaurants`, `Orders`, `Menu Items`, `Reviews`, `Payments`, `Delivery Partners`, and `Addresses`.  

I defined relationships between entities such as:  
- Users placing orders.  
- Restaurants offering menu items.  
- Delivery partners handling deliveries.  

Key attributes discussed included **ratings**, **order status**, and **payment details**.

## HR Round (30 Minutes)

### Questions Asked  
- Introduce yourself.  
- Rate the overall interview out of 10.  
- Where do you see yourself in the next 5 years?  
- Why do you want to work with HashedIn?  
- Explain the difference between **SQL** and **NoSQL** databases.  

### Task  
I was also asked to **play a Tic-Tac-Toe game**, demonstrate the **game logic**, handle **player moves**, and determine **win or draw** conditions.  

---

## Key Takeaways  

- Focus on **DSA concepts** (especially arrays, linked lists, and dynamic programming).  
- Have a clear understanding of **system design basics** like caching, load balancing, and databases.  
- Brush up on **ER diagrams** and **real-world design problems**.  
- Be prepared for **logical games or coding puzzles** even in HR rounds.
